## 🧠 About Me

I’m interested in building machine learning systems that solve real-world problems, particularly in healthcare and biological domains.

My focus lies in working with structured and unstructured data, designing reliable pipelines, and understanding how models behave beyond just performance metrics.

---

## 🔬 Interests

- Machine learning applications in **drug discovery and bioinformatics**
- Computer vision for **medical imaging and diagnostics**
- Multimodal systems combining **clinical, biological, and imaging data**
- Model evaluation, metric selection, and error analysis in high-stakes domains

---

## ⚙️ What I Like Working On

- Projects that involve **real-world, imperfect datasets**
- Building **end-to-end pipelines** from data to model
- Problems where **evaluation and decision-making matter more than accuracy**
- Systems that require **understanding trade-offs**, not just implementation

---

## 📈 Looking For

- Opportunities in **applied ML research**
- Work in **healthcare AI, medical imaging, or computational biology**
- Collaborations on projects involving **real-world datasets and impact-driven problems**
